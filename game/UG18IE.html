Blocky Dude Gangster Auto City

You are the target of a grand gangster crime. Your mission is to go into the city and take control of the situation. You are the leader of a gang of criminals and you have to make sure that the gangster crime doesn't get out of hand. To accomplish this, you have to go on a journey of crime and become the king of the gangsters. You will have to deal with various gangs and their bosses. It is up to you to choose which gang you want to work with. There is a lot of freedom in the game and you can decide how the story will go. The gameplay is in a third-person perspective and the game world is open. You can explore the world from the first mission and you can go anywhere. The gameplay is action-packed and the missions will keep you on your toes.
